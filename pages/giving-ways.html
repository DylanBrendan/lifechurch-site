Campus Giving

You can make your gifts in person using cash, checks, or debit/credit cards.

While you’re attending experiences at LifeChurch.tv, you can use giving envelopes or giving drop boxes for your convenience and security.

You can always drop off or mail a check to your campus office.

Mobile Smart Giving

You can give tithes and offerings to LifeChurch.tv via text message.

We'll link your mobile phone number with the credit/debit card you provide, and you'll be set up to give securely, easily, and instantly.

  • Link a credit or debit card
  • As easy as sending a text
  • Tithe or give to specific causes

Non-Cash Giving

Did you know you can donate non-cash gifts like stocks, bonds, mutual funds, or cars? There are many other ways to give:

  • Marketable Securities: transfer ownership of your stocks, bonds, or mutual fund shares
  • Other Non-Cash Gifts: donate items like cars, boats, and jewelry
  • Life Insurance: name LifeChurch.tv as a beneficiary of your policy
  • Planned Gifts: give via retirement assets, bequests, trusts, etc. as part of your overall personal financial strategy
