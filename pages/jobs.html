When you ask us what we do at LifeChurch.tv you won’t just get a job description — you’ll hear a story.

We are passionate about using our talents for God’s purposes and that’s why we’ve dedicated our careers to a ministry, not a job title.

Campus

LifeChurch.tv is a multi-site church with locations, or campuses, all over the United States. Each campus has a dedicated team of pastors, administrators, and leaders who minister directly to the people who attend our church.
