Way more than a paycheck.

So, you’ve been called to ministry. You’ve learned a little more about us – our values and our stories. There’s an opening that seems just right for you. But it’s so much more than that. There’s no such thing as a cushy job at LifeChurch.tv, so we go the extra mile to support you. We’ve listed our favorite perks below or you can download a copy for more details.

Development Culture

When you get better, we get better. So instead of a development program, we have a development culture. Continual learning and growth are part of being on the team, and you’ll have opportunities, support, and plenty of feedback to help you grow professionally and spiritually.

Team Appreciation Events & Retreats

We believe in expressing gratitude for a job well done. We also think it’s important for teams to be on the same page. Many teams take time together away from the office to learn, get to know each other better, celebrate, and share ideas for what’s ahead.

Competitive 401K

We want to manage money well, both as a church and on our own. We have a matching 401K plan and make contributions on your behalf even if you don’t pay in.

Dress Code: Come Dressed

Just like our church services, our dress code is simple: please do! Wear whatever makes you (and the people around you) ready to get the job done.

Mobile Phone Allowance

You make and take calls for work on your mobile phone, so, we reimburse staff for expenses incurred as part of your role at LifeChurch.tv.

Technology That Works as Hard as You Do

You’ll have the tools you need to do your job with excellence. Just like we use technology in our efforts to reach people for Christ, you’ll be well equipped for your role.

Staff Development Dollars

We expect you to learn, but you know how you learn best. Each team member has a personal budget to use toward resources like books, training seminars, and workshops.

Flexible Vacation/Leave Plans

Working hard for something you believe in is incredibly rewarding. It also takes a lot of energy, so you’ll have plenty of time off for relaxing and recharging.

Wellness Reimbursement

We care about your well-being, so you’ll receive quarterly reimbursements for gym memberships, sporting fees, fitness equipment and more.

Work Hard - Have Fun

There’s a time to work hard, then there’s a time to play hard. And we like doing both with our LifeChurch.tv family. We learn, play, and laugh together on a regular basis.

The perk that can’t be beat: Leading People to Become Fully Devoted Followers of Christ.
