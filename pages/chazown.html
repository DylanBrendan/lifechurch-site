The Chazown Experience

Uncover and pursue God's dream for your life.

Each of us is a masterpiece, placed on earth for a unique purpose that's solely ours to fulfill.

Through this interactive campus event, you will examine your core values, spiritual gifts, and past experiences. From these three areas, you’ll be able to identify some common themes and messages that will bring clarity to the unique passions and purpose God has given you. Then, you can plan some next steps to help you start living out God’s vision, or chazown, for your life.
