Doing better at doing good.

Every person on this earth matters to God. And we are able to show His love to people through our relief and restoration efforts.

At LifeChurch.tv, we work to continually gain perspective on the most effective ways to help those in need—in our communities and around the world. Learn more about our approach to meeting the needs of others through Restore.

A key part of our approach includes investing time and people through Local Missions Partners and investing resources in our Global Missions Partners. When you give beyond the tithe to Relief and Restoration at LifeChurch.tv, you can be confident that you providing disaster relief and supporting restorative relationships worldwide.

Convoy of Hope

Convoy of Hope's disaster relief efforts are successful due to their emphasis on excellence in logistics, and their strong alliances with state, national, and international relief agencies.

All Convoy of Hope disaster relief efforts occur in partnership with local churches, so that disaster victims have the opportunity for ongoing connection with a local church. Convoy of Hope has responded to disasters in over 109 countries around the world.

Compassion International's Child Survival Programs

Compassion International’s existing church partners run over 400 Child Survival Programs (CSP) in 15 countries around the world.

CSPs reach out to parents with small children, from pregnancy through age 5, and provide education about child development, health and nutrition, spiritual/biblical training, and basic job skills training.

LifeChurch.tv fully funds the costs of CSPs in Ecuador, Uganda, the Philippines, India, which represents over 500 parents and children.

NewMissions

Only 20% of children in Haiti have access to education. NewMissions addresses poverty by building and running churches and schools throughout Haiti.

Each day, approximately 11,000 children attend school, receive a hot meal, medical care, school books, school uniforms, and the opportunity to hear the Gospel message of hope that changes lives. LifeChurch.tv helps to fund a school/church for more than 360 students as well as the construction of a trade school in Calitor, Haiti.

HOPE International

HOPE International focuses on alleviating physical and spiritual poverty through microenterprise development in 16 countries around the world.

HOPE International programs provide Christ-centered microfinance and savings, along with education. Microfinance isn't a handout but a hand-up. It requires ownership and active participation from the beneficiaries. Microloan recipients can take pride in knowing that their own hard work has made the difference between poverty and provision for their families.

LifeChurch.tv provides support to Hope International’s global work.

The Chalmers Center

The Chalmers Center's unique approach to microfinance combines cutting-edge, savings-led strategies with education for people who are poor about their businesses, households, and health.

Using a relational, biblical worldview approach, Microfinance With Education is a powerful intervention that even the poorest churches in the Majority World can use to respond to the complexities of poverty in their communities. LifeChurch.tv fully funds a Mobile Training Center in the country of Togo in West Africa.
